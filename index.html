It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks pioneered the concept of a relaxed coffeehouse where people could enjoy quality coffee. Before Starbucks, coffee was mainly served in diners and other eateries. The brand’s transformation from a coffee beans store to a coffee house created a unique space for coffee lovers. Today, Starbucks remains the world’s largest coffee chain, with fans loyal to its name
